About Us

Mukti Healthcare has been serving the community for over 15 years with a mission to provide affordable, compassionate, and high-quality medical care. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by a team of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and support staff.

We believe in a patient-first approach, where every individual is treated with dignity and respect. From preventive health check-ups to advanced surgical procedures, we cover a wide spectrum of healthcare needs under one roof.
